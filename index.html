New York Times Article Extract Tool

Extract Article
To install, drag and drop the above item to your bookmarks bar.

Introduction

This tool can help you to extract New York Times article in a very simple way.
This tool is very useful when you want to print a New York Times article without other elements on their webpage.
It also has an unexpected feature that it can bypass New York Times free article limitation.

Installation

Just drag and drop the above button to your bookmarks bar.

Usage

  1. Go to a New York Times article webpage like this.
  2. Click the extract tool bookmark on your bookmarks bar.
  3. Enjoy your fresh New York Times article.
